Greenhouse Gases

Carbon dioxide, methane, nitrous oxide, and other gases stick around in our atmosphere and increase average global temperature in four steps.

  1. Visible solar radiation is absorbed by the Earth, which heats up
  2. The Earth radiates infrared radiation to space
  3. Greenhouse gases absorb and re-emit some infrared radiation back to Earth
  4. Abnormal atmospheric levels of greenhouse gases can keep more thermal energy in the troposphere

Global Climate Change

Primarily caused by average temperature increase, climates are changing worldwide. Listed here are just a few of the direct and indirect effects of global climate change.

  • Glaciers melt
  • Sea levels rise
